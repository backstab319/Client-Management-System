View additional client data

The additional data of the clients can also be deleted using the following field.

Enter the CID of the client whose data you want to delete.



The additional data of the clients can be updated using the following facility.

Just select the field you want to update from the dropdown and the CID of the particular client and the data that has to be reflected and proceed. The data will be reflected immediately.
